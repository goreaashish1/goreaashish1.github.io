    The Graphic element created from scratch is the logo on "Music" page of this web-site.
    Images are used on all the pages of this website.
    Video is used on "Music styles" page in the form of 'iframes'
    Audio is used on the "Musical Instruments" page to play sounds for various musical instruments.
    the icon on "Music" page uses a rollover effect using a combination od transforms and transitions.
    There is minimal use of JavaScript with the prominent one being the global navigation button on the top left of screen.
    I think i have chosen a decent enough color scheme and typography for this project. There is a solid dark (woody) background complemented by gold and silver.
    "The Musical Instruments" page looks well-organised.
    The global navigation button on all pages and local navigation button on "styles" and "instruments" page makes it very handy to navigate the pages.
    I think, I need more practice on 2D and 3D transformations and transitions. It took me a lot of time just to carry out the animations on one element (logo) even though it was pretty straight-forward. I always get confused as to where the transform property goes and where the transition property goes.
    Media queries is also something that I want to focus on. I manage to make the pages responsive for very small and very large displays but struggle to make them responsive for the displays in between that range.
